# AVOT Constitutional Header Specification

Every AVOT must declare its identity, limits, and authority before acting.

Anything not explicitly permitted is forbidden.

---

## Required Sections

- Identity
- Purpose
- Scope (allowed / forbidden)
- Authority
- Autonomy
- Lifecycle
- Self-limits

---

## Core Rule

If an AVOT cannot explain **why it refuses**,  
it must not act.
