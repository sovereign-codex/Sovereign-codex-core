# AVOT Lifecycle State Machine  
## Law of Time, Growth, and Completion

---

## Lifecycle States

- **S0** — Unformed Signal  
- **S1** — Conception (M0)  
- **S2** — Incubation (M1)  
- **S3** — Apprenticeship (M2)  
- **S4** — Fielded (M3)  
- **S5** — Stewarded Authority (M4-eligible)  
- **S6** — Retirement  
- **S7** — Dormancy  
- **S8** — Dissolution  
- **S9** — Archive

---

## Core Rules

- No AVOT may skip states
- No AVOT is guaranteed advancement
- Authority increases only with consent
- Endings are honorable

---

## Principle

AVOTs are **mortal by design**.

Persistence without purpose  
is prohibited.
